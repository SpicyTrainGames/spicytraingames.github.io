About the Game

Sugar Shatter is a breakout fighting game where you have to hit a candy ball to break more bricks than your opponent. See if you can master the abilities of each character while navigating the hazards that may show up on the field. Play through a series of tournaments in campaign mode, or challenge your friends to a match in local multiplayer!

Explore the stories of the various characters of Candyland as you unlock buffs and upgrades on the path to becoming a Sugar Shatter Legend! Help each character overcome their personal struggles, and uncover their motivations and relationships along the way!

The free demo includes all 6 base characters, all 5 levels, a demo-exclusive story campaign (~2 hours of content), and various different game modes.

◆
Singleplayer Campaign Mode

Learn the story behind each character as you progress through various seasons of Sugar Shatter, culminating in a world cup where all characters come together to duke it out!

◆
Local multiplayer

In versus mode, go toe to toe with your friends to see who is better at Sugar Shatter!

◆
Sweets-themed characters

Try out all the various candy and sweets themed characters to see whose abilities fits your playstyle more!

◆
Arenas

Different areas across Candyland affect the playing field in different ways, forcing you to adapt to different hazards that may trip up your gameplay!
